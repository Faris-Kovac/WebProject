Welcome TO Sarajevo Limo Service

Welcome to our transfer service company, situated in the bustling heart of Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.
Our pledge is to deliver unparalleled service built on reliability and accountability. Our drivers uphold industry-leading standards, ensuring a seamless experience.

Embracing innovation and contemporary trends, we foster a culture of shared vision and excellence within our team, securing our position as market leaders. Count on our drivers for genuine warmth, adaptability, and support, whether navigating intricate addresses or assisting with luggage. Proficient in English and knowledgeable about our country's rich history, our drivers offer more than just transportation.

As a pet-friendly company, we specialize in EU pet transportation alongside transparent, fixed pricing, with no hidden fees. Our service quality is endorsed by top hotels in Sarajevo and across Bosnia and Herzegovina, providing direct transfers for renowned hotel groups like Marriott (Courtyard, Residence Inn) and Accor (Swissotel, Novotel, Ibis Styles).

Our client roster includes prestigious entities such as the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, Intesa Sanpaolo Bank, and Amsterdam Rechtbank – De. We tailor our pricing and services to meet the varied needs of all clients, offering a diverse range of vehicle classes - from standard sedans and upper-middle-class business sedans to premium sedans and executive chauffeur services.

Connect with us, and let our team curate the perfect solution for your needs effortlessly. Experience the epitome of premium transportation with us.
